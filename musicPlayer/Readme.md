# 2. About project

![sample](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/129382685/230964863-1195af99-3efb-4298-a0e7-50d1d06e1872.png)

### Why choose this project?

I usually listening music by youtube, not youtube music. Whenever I want to add some video that I liked, I add to my playlist. But when I changed my mind, or my taste, I have to delete whole videos of my playlist or I have to make new. I wanted to create a new disposable YouTube playlist of my own every day. This is a feature where the video automatically gets removed from the playlist once it finishes playing, and you can rearrange or partially delete the queued playlist. The changed order or queued list remains unchanged even after refreshing, and the next video is played immediately after the currently playing video ends. Perfect fit for my usage.
